ABOUT US

Spreading the warmth & love of authentic italian food over 30 years, Joyvilla is India’s favourite italian restaurant. Italian food is simple yet sophisticated, using few ingredients to make a dish so flavourful you keep coming back for more. Inspired by Italy’s finest family chefs, we bring you the warmth & love of Italian Cuisine directly to your home.

Inspired by traditional Italian cooking & processes we at Little Italy offer a menu backed by the finest Italian chefs & quality ingredients.We have our own cheese factory and source our ingredients from suppliers that offer the freshest produce, making it possible for us to serve you, the Italian way.

We invite you to join us on this journey of spreading love & warmth through authentic italian food!
